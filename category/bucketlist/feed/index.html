]]> We got our international flights about 3 months ago. We are flying out of LAX via EVA Air. See y’all on the other side!

The best way to find cheap tickets is word-of-mouth, but when that fails, I’ve found SkyScanner and Google Flights to be the best sites for the cheapest airfares. (Remember to check SouthWest if you’re flying USA domestic, they don’t use any third party dealers.) SkyScanner was able to find Danielle and I tickets from Thailand to Vietnam from $22 each. I don’t know if that’s a good deal yet, but it sounded great to me.
